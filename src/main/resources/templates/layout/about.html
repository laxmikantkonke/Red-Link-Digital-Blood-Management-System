About RED-LINK

Connecting donors with those in need, creating a lifeline for the community.

RED-LINK is a comprehensive blood management system designed to bridge the gap between blood donors, hospitals, and patients. Our mission is to ensure that no life is lost due to the unavailability of blood.

We provide a platform where donors can find nearby hospitals, recipients can request blood, and hospitals can manage their inventory efficiently.

Our Mission

To save lives by simplifying the process of blood donation and request.

Our Vision

A world where safe blood is available to everyone, everywhere, instantly.

Our Community

Thousands of heroes who donate blood and save lives every day.
